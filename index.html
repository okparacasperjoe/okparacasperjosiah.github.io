HOW WE MET…

How I met my husband

BRIDE (RUTH DURU EZINNE)

Talking about how we met, what quickly comes to mind is the 2016 batch A NCCF baptismal rural rugged that held in plateau state where we were posted to serve our father’s land.

We were part of the corpers who embarked on the outreach. However, I only noticed ‘uncle’ on the day of our return. He had playfully walked up to me, collected my phone and wanted to take some of my pictures which I vehemently disagreed to. I didn’t know I had an admirer already; I was just forming one serious ‘aunty’…lolzz. I’m sure my response threw him aback. Hence, we all went our separate ways to serve in different villages though in the same local government.

Fast forward to few months into service, we met again at the zonal excos handover service only to discover that we’ll be serving as executives under the NCCF. ‘Uncle’ was appointed the zonal coordinator while I became the unit coordinator and later a sub-zonal coordinator. Uncle was simply a diplomatic and democratic leader. He visited a couple of times on official grounds and that was just it…

Serving in different capacities in a team eventually made us friends and this friendship was maintained even after the national youth service. It’s a few years down the line and off course, like it always happens, just one faithful day I was with ‘uncle’ and after saying one or two lines, he popped the popular question on a bended knee – “will you marry me?”… So that’s where we are now oh.
